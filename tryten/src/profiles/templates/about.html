ATS overview

Many people apply for jobs online these days -as numbers increases, it becomes more and more challenging to maintain the work flow of the recruiting process for the company offering these jobs. An Applicant Tracking System is a module that keeps track of the process of on boarding the applicants in to a firm. We are planning to design an ATS from the ground-up that can perform tasks such as: Maintain Applicant Database, search resumes, find the right applicant for a position based the no of matches in their skill set to the job requirements and keep the track of the dates for various events involved. .
